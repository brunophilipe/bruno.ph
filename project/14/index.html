Nixie Watch

Nixie-tube watch “face” for Apple Watch.

watchOS, Swift, CoreImage

When I saw Woz demonstrating his “Nixie-Tube wrist watch”, a behemoth that makes the Apple watch look like futuristic miniature tech, I decided it would be a good watch face to develop. However once it became clear Apple doesn’t want developers to create third party faces, I decided to simply make it anyway and put it on GitHub.

It was quite a challenge to build, in the end, since watchOS (especially watchOS 2, at the time) has very limited graphics capabilities. I had to render the watch face in memory using the CoreGraphics, output that to an image to later display in the screen.

It is a project I go over every once in a while and change some small things, and it certainly makes people curious when I show them my “exclusive” watch face.
